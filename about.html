About Neighborhood Navigator

Our Mission

Neighborhood Navigator was created to help people explore new locations in an organized and meaningful way. Whether you are in the relocating phase or researching phase, this application will provide structured information to help guide your decisions.

Key Fearures

Neighborhood Navigator brings together information such as schools, healthcare, transportation, and lifestyle ameneties into one structured, easy-to-navigate space to help make your research more efficient.

  • Search for any city using our live geolcation search feature
  • Explore featured cities with detailed summaries
  • Save locations to a personalized profile
  • Share community tips through the "Wish I Knew" page
